The iconic Laie, Hawaii Temple of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints was first dedicated by Heber J. Grant in 1919 and has been rennovated and rededicated twice since. This was the first temple to be dedicated outside of Utah once the saints had settled there.

Our Purpose

As our motto says, we are striving to provide you with a heavenly experience as you attend or visit the temple. With nearly 50 locations, each adjacent a temple site, we are working to give temple patrons a place to change, relax, lodge for the night, learn more about the temple, or to be a home base for the many other activites available in the surrounding areas.
