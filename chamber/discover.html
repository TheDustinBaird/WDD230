Discover Milky Mountain

Walk on the Wild Side

There are several amazing areas to explore locally with both guided and unguided adventures.

Taste the Town

Let us cook dinner tonight. Discover one of our many local restauraunts. Bring the family or enjoy a quiet date night.

Hit the Slopes

Milky Mountain Ski Resort will WOW you with its majestic views and hundreds of miles of terrain to explore.

Rodeo

Hope you brought your boots and hat, there's a rodeo every six weeks. Check the schedule here.

Bear World

Bear World is just a short drive away. Meander through this natural habitat and interact with hundreds of bears.

Fish the Local Rivers

You've never seen fly fishing like this. Check out our world-class rivers and streams.

Visit the Shops

Swing by the shops on Main Street and see what's new and especially what's old.

The Theater

Our local theater is a great place to spend a relaxing evening with someone special.

Taxidermy Museum

If dead, stuffed animals are your thing we've got just the place for you. Come find yourself a new friend.
